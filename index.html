What is Tombstone?

The Tombstone Squad is, as the name says, a squad. Our squad curently exists of 11 people and we play especially Rainbow Six Siege. The name 'Tombstone' origins from a squad in the single player mode, from "Battlefield 4". Tombstone Squad was created in early 2015 by Shihaab Rouine & Casper Steeg.
